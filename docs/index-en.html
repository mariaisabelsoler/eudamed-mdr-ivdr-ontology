EUDAMED MDR-IVDR Ontology: Description back to ToC

The purpose of the ontology is to model the domain of medical devices and in vitro diagnostic devices registered in the European EUDAMED database, including manufacturers, identified risks, incidents, and field safety corrective actions (FSCAs). The goal is to enable the different information systems in the sector — manufacturers, regulatory authorities, hospitals, and patients — to share data accurately, without ambiguity, and to interoperate easily with each other.

  • MDR enforces MDRRiskClass

  • MedicalDevice Type by isActive
  • MedicalDevice Type by isImplantable
  • MedicalDevice Type by isReusable

CorrectiveAction Status by closureDateback to ToC or SWRL ToC

Marks a corrective action as Closed when it has a closure date.

Corrective Action (FSCA) c(?a) , closure date dp(?a,?date) -> status op(?a,?Closed)

IVDR enforces IVDRRiskClassback to ToC or SWRL ToC

If a device has IVDR as regulatory framework, its risk class must be of type IVDRRiskClass.

Medical Device c(?d) , has regulatory framework op(?d,?IVDR) , has risk class op(?d,?rc) -> IVDR Risk Class c(?rc)

MDR enforces MDRRiskClassback to ToC or SWRL ToC

If a device has MDR as regulatory framework, its risk class must be of type MDRRiskClass.

Medical Device c(?d) , has regulatory framework op(?d,?MDR) , has risk class op(?d,?rc) -> MDR Risk Class c(?rc)

MedicalDevice RegulatoryFramework by isIvdback to ToC or SWRL ToC

Assigns MDR regulatory framework to devices that are not in vitro diagnostic.

is IVD dp(?d,"false"^^boolean) , Medical Device c(?d) -> has regulatory framework op(?d,?MDR)

is active dp(?d,"true"^^boolean) , Medical Device c(?d) -> Active Device c(?d)
